# Scenario 03: Calendar Conflict

## Executive Context
You support an executive who wants client-facing meetings protected whenever possible.

## Fake Inbox
1. Client Success Director asks if executive can still attend the 3:00 PM client review call.
2. Internal Leadership Team has an internal planning meeting also at 3:00 PM.
3. Executive says: “Please make sure I do not miss anything client-critical today.”
4. Vendor Partner offers an optional product demo at 3:00 PM.

## Learner Tasks
1. Identify which meeting should be prioritized.
2. Draft a reply to the Client Success Director.
3. Draft a note to the executive summarizing the conflict.
4. Decide whether the vendor demo should be accepted, declined, or rescheduled.
