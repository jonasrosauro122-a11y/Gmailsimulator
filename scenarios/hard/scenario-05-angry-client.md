# Scenario 05: Angry Client Email

## Executive Context
You support an executive who wants all client complaints handled calmly and escalated before any promises are made.

## Fake Inbox

### Email 1
**From:** Angela Brooks, Client  
**Subject:** Very Disappointed With the Delay

This is unacceptable. We were told we would receive an update yesterday, and no one followed through. I need an answer today.

### Email 2
**From:** Account Manager  
**Subject:** RE: Client Delay

We are still waiting on final confirmation from operations. Please do not promise completion yet.

### Email 3
**From:** Operations Team  
**Subject:** Update Pending

We need two more hours to verify the final status.

## Learner Tasks
1. Mark the client email as urgent.
2. Draft a calm acknowledgment reply.
3. Do not promise completion.
4. Escalate to the executive and Account Manager.
5. Write a follow-up reminder to operations.
