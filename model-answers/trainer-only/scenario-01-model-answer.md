# Scenario 01 Model Answer

## Recommended Triage
| Email No. | Priority | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Urgent | Reply and check calendar availability |
| 2 | FYI | No reply needed |
| 3 | Needs Executive Approval | Escalate before approving |
| 4 | Important | Send follow-up response |
| 5 | Needs Escalation | Notify executive of calendar conflict |

## Sample Reply to Client Reschedule
Subject: Re: Meeting Reschedule Request

Hi Sarah,

Thank you for reaching out. I understand that you need to reschedule today’s meeting.

I will check Madam Alicia’s calendar and confirm the next available time as soon as possible.

Thank you,  
[Name]
