# Learner Guide

## Goal
Show that you can manage an executive inbox with accuracy, urgency, and professionalism.

## Practice Skills
- Identify urgent emails
- Separate FYI emails from action-required emails
- Draft polished replies
- Escalate sensitive concerns
- Avoid casual language
- Use a clear subject line

## Email Reply Formula
Greeting → Acknowledgment → Clear action/update → Next step → Professional closing

## Avoid
`lol`, `haha`, `u`, `asap!!!`, unclear replies, emotional tone, and unauthorized promises.
