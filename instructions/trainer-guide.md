# Trainer Guide

- Professional tone
- Completeness

- Follow-up quality

## Score Levels
- 95–100%: Executive Ready
- 90–94%: Passed
- 80–89%: Needs Minor Coaching
- 70–79%: Needs Retraining
- Below 70%: Repeat Scenario
